> `[由Gemini gemini-3.1-flash-lite-preview 识别]`
Hello Siyuan,
I'm writing you from my town Voerendaal, in the south of The Netherlands. It has a population of +/- 12,500 people.
It is very chilly here. At the moment 3°C (12:4 hour). Since this week the frost set in at night. Brrr...
We are getting ready to celebrate Sinterklaas (Saint Nicholas) on December 5. It is a fest for children, unfortunately it was on international news because some people think that his happy helpers Zwarte Pieten (Black Petes) are racism. Believe me, anyone who celebrate this fest for children doesn't agree to that. Maybe you heard something about it.
My daughter (3) likes Sinterklaas (Saint Nicholas) and the Zwarte Pieten (Black Petes) very much. It is a beautiful tradition where we lie to kids the same as we do about Santa Claus! 😊
Groetjes (Greetings) Tamara

* 翻译：

> `[由Gemini gemini-3.1-flash-lite-preview 翻译]`
你好冯思远，
我正从荷兰南部的小镇福伦达尔（Voerendaal）给你写信。这里大约有一万两千五百名居民。
这里天气非常冷，目前是摄氏 3 度。从这周开始，夜里已经结霜了。冷得发抖……
我们正准备庆祝 12 月 5 日的圣尼古拉斯节（Sinterklaas）。这是一个属于孩子们的节日，遗憾的是它上了国际新闻，因为有些人认为他那些快乐的帮手“黑彼得”（Zwarte Pieten）带有种族主义色彩。相信我，任何为孩子们庆祝这个节日的人都不会同意这种说法。也许你听说过这件事。
我三岁的女儿非常喜欢圣尼古拉斯和“黑彼得”。这是一个美丽的传统，我们像对待圣诞老人一样对孩子们编织着美丽的谎言！😊
Groetjes（问候）Tamara
